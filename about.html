Our Story

Empowering Women
Across West Africa

KIKI HAIR was founded with one mission: to make world-class virgin human hair accessible to every woman in West Africa — without the premium price tag or the wait.

We noticed that our sisters across Nigeria, Ghana, Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire and beyond were spending too much on low-quality hair, or waiting weeks for unreliable deliveries. We decided to change that.

Today, KIKI HAIR is West Africa's most trusted premium hair brand, serving over 5,000 clients across 15+ countries. Every strand we sell is hand-inspected, ethically sourced, and backed by our quality guarantee.

Our Mission

We believe every woman deserves to feel powerful, beautiful, and confident — and the right hair is a big part of that. Our mission is to provide the highest quality virgin human hair at fair prices, with fast and reliable delivery across West Africa.

We source our hair ethically from trusted suppliers around the world — Brazil, India, Malaysia, and Peru — ensuring that every strand meets our strict quality standards before reaching you.

🌱

Ethical Sourcing

We work only with ethical suppliers who ensure fair treatment and compensation for all hair donors.

🔬

Quality Tested

Every batch goes through rigorous quality checks. We test for shedding, tangling, and texture consistency before shipping.

❤️

Client Obsessed

Our 98% satisfaction rate isn't luck — it's the result of genuinely caring about every client's experience, from order to delivery.
