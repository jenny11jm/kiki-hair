💎 Product Quality

Is your hair 100% human hair?

+
Yes, absolutely! All our hair is 100% virgin human hair — no synthetic blends, no chemical processing. Our hair is sourced ethically from single donors and is unprocessed, meaning all cuticles are intact and aligned in the same direction. This is why our hair is tangle-free, shed-free, and incredibly soft.

Can I color or bleach the hair?

+
Yes! Because our hair is 100% virgin human hair, it can be colored, bleached, permed, and heat-styled just like your natural hair. We recommend doing a patch test first and using professional-grade products. For bleaching, we suggest going 1–2 shades at a time to maintain hair health. Always deep condition after coloring.

How long will the hair last?

+
With proper care, our premium virgin hair lasts 1–3 years. Many of our loyal clients have used their KIKI HAIR bundles for 2+ years! The key is regular deep conditioning, gentle detangling, sleeping with a silk bonnet, and avoiding excessive heat. We include a full hair care guide with every order.

What hair textures do you offer?

+
We offer a wide range of textures: Straight, Body Wave, Deep Wave, Loose Deep Wave, Water Wave, Deep Curly, Kinky Curly, and Natural Wave. All textures are available in multiple lengths from 8 to 40 inches. We also offer colored options including ombre, highlights, and 613 blonde.

✂️ Hair Care

How do I care for my KIKI HAIR bundles?

+
Caring for your KIKI HAIR is simple: (1) Wash gently with sulfate-free shampoo every 1–2 weeks. (2) Deep condition after every wash. (3) Detangle gently from ends to roots with a wide-tooth comb. (4) Air dry when possible; use low heat settings when blow-drying. (5) Sleep with a silk or satin bonnet/pillowcase. (6) Avoid excessive chlorine or salt water exposure. Every order includes our full care guide PDF!

How many bundles do I need?

+
It depends on the length and density you want: For short styles (10–14 inch): 2 bundles. For medium styles (16–20 inch): 2–3 bundles. For long styles (22–28 inch): 3–4 bundles. For extra long/full styles (30+ inch): 4–5 bundles. If you're adding a closure or frontal, you typically need 1 fewer bundle. Message us on WhatsApp and we'll help you figure out exactly what you need!
